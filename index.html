1. Upload a video

Easily upload your video from your local computer, and wait a few moments for your video to be processed.
 
Once complete, start transcribing!

2. Select and transcribe

Choose your AI, how exactly you want the AI to transcribe your video, and preview your settings on the video itself!
 
Satisfied? Let the AI work its magic.

3. Review and edit

Keep track of the transcribed text which has been time sectioned on the right panel for easy playback.
 
Finetune as needed by editing directly into the textarea.

4. Save and download

Once done, you can save your edits and download the subtitles to your local computer!
